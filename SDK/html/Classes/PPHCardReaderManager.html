Overview

The card reader manager handles all interaction with card and chip&pin hardware devices. This includes audio readers, dock port readers, and Bluetooth readers.

Each reader goes through 5 states during it’s lifecycle:

Available - A potential reader of one of the monitored types has been detected. Connecting - We are in the process of acquiring more information about the reader. Connected - The reader is fully identified. Open - The reader is listening for card data
