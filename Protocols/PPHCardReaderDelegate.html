PPHCardReaderDelegate Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in PPHCardReaderDelegate.h

Overview

All events related to the card reader – audio, chip & pin bluetooth, etc.

Tasks

  • – didDetectReaderDevice:

    A fully working reader was detected and is available
  • – didDetectUpgradeableReader:withMessage:isRequired:

    Certain reader types are field upgradeable with things like keys and software updates. If you get this message, you will NOT receive didDetectReaderDevice until the condition is resolved.
  • – didUpgradeReader:withMessage:

    A reader upgrade was successful
  • – didReceiveReaderUpgradeStatusWithMessage:currentStep:totalSteps:completion:

    There is a status update to the current ongoing reader upgrade
  • – didRemoveReader:

    A reader device has been removed from the system
  • – didStartReaderDetection:

    This event will be triggered in cases where reader detection takes a while, such as for the audio readers. It presents an opportunity to show UI indicating that you are “working on it”
  • – didDetectCardSwipeAttempt

    Something has occurred in the read head of the reader. Since processing can take a second or so, this allows you to get some UI up. Be careful how much work you do here because taxing the CPU will hurt success rate.
  • – didCompleteCardSwipe:

    A card swipe has succeeded
  • – didFailToReadCard

    A swipe attempt failed. Usually this means the magstripe could not be read and the merchant should try again.
  • – didReceiveCardReaderMetadata:

    Serial number and other related information is available for the card reader.
  • – didReceiveChipAndPinEvent:

    Called when an event related to chip & pin transactions has occurred on the reader
